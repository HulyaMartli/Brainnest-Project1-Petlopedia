Petlopedia is a pet encyclopedia for pet enthusiasts!

Pets have become an indispensable part of our lives throughout history. They
are members of our families, our most beloved ones, and our babies. As their
companion, we are always trying to learn more about them, explore their
mysterious world, and be the best partner we can be for them.

I am Hulya Martli, an animal lover who is always fascinated with learning
more about our companions' world. I always try to keep up with the latest

knowledge, and therefore I created Petlopedia. You can learn more about our
most beloved friends' species history, breeds, behaviors, and much more here!
